Coldplay

A British rock band that was formed in London in 1996. Vocalist and pianist Chris Martin, guitarist Jonny Buckland, bassist Guy Berryman, and drummer Will Champion.

Oasis

Oasis developed from an earlier group, the Rain. Upon returning to Manchester, Liam's older brother, Noel Gallagher joined as a fifth member. They're famous for "Wonderwall"

Arctic Monkeys

An English rock band formed in Sheffield in 2002. The group consists of Alex Turner, Jamie Cook, Nick O'Malley, and Matt Helders. They have released six studio albums and they're ready to rock!
